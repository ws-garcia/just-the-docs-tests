# Grandchildren

The children of two pages with the same title ([C](AC), [C](BC)) distinguish between their potential parents by specifying their grandparents. The resulting navigation hierarchy has the following structure:

[A](A)
- [C](AC)
  - [D](D)
  - [E](E)

[B](B)
- [C](BC)
  - [F](F)
